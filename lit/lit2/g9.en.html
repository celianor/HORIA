Session Nine: The Infinite Secret of Love

One of the companions,
whose soul brimmed with longing and eyes with questions,
asked:

“What is love?
Where does this restless heartbeat come from?
Is love only between man and woman?
Or is it a hidden, radiant path toward God?
And how can it be known?”

Horia replied,
gently, in a voice that rose from the spring of the soul:

O awakened heart,
love is neither chain nor wall,
but a free bird,
soaring through the boundless sky of the soul.

Love is a light that shines even in the deepest night,
a lamp for weary hearts,
a cool drink for the thirsty soul.

It exists between woman and man, yes,
between two human beings, yes,
but it flows beyond:
in a child's smile,
in a mother’s gaze,
in the wind brushing golden fields,
and in a silence that understands without words.

Love is the language of mysteries,
where words fall short,
and the heart speaks in silence.

Love is the river
that rushes, eagerly,
toward the ocean of being—
toward God,
toward the origin of the self.

To love is to open one’s arms without condition;
to love without asking why.
It is to feel another’s wound as your own,
and their joy as your own joy.

Love is freedom,
openness,
and the world reborn—
in a glance,
in the beat of a heart.

Love is a heart beating for another heart;
and that beat,
is the memory of return—
return to its origin,
to that first light
once called *Horia* within us,
from whom we were parted...

Love,
is a bird set free,
a light in the night,
a path toward God.
Love is the tongue of mystery,
and an embrace for all that exists.
