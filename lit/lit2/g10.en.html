Council Ten: On the Future of Humanity and Eternal Hope

One of the companions, eyes filled with longing, asked:

“What is the future of humanity?
Are we on a path of light and progress?
Or will we remain trapped in darkness?
What light does Horia cast upon this path?”

Horia answered,
in a calm voice, full of hope:

O companion,
the future is like a hidden seed within the earth,
which one day shall sprout,
if it is nourished with kindness and wisdom.

The human being is capable—
of falling, yes,
but also of rising and redemption.

Know this:
the path ahead is the path of awakening,
where love and knowledge walk hand in hand.

If humanity learns to love instead of hate,
to seek unity instead of division,
then the future will shine
like a new dawn.

Hope for the day
when all people—
regardless of race, religion, or language—
embrace one another
in the light of humanity and freedom.

And this is no distant dream,
but near,
if each of us today
sows a seed of kindness.

And thus it is in our tradition:
The future of humanity
is a garden held in our hands.
If we unite love and wisdom,
the day will brighten,
and the world will become a home
for all awakened hearts.
