Twelfth Assembly: On Wisdom and the Light of the Heart

O kind companion and fellow traveler,
Wisdom is not merely dry and cold knowledge,
nor just a collection of words written on ancient pages—
but a heavenly light born deep within the heart,
a sun that rises in the soul’s chest,
dispelling the shadows of fear and ignorance.

Wisdom is listening to the voiceless whispers of the soul,
hearing those soft melodies that reason often fails to grasp;
it is a gaze that sees beyond the surface of things,
opening the eye of the heart to behold the unseen threads that bind all things together—
threads flowing like blood through the living body of existence,
weaving everything into a single, endless tapestry.

Wisdom is a lamp that glows in the darkest nights of life,
illuminating the path of a lost soul,
unfastening, one by one, the chains of confusion and ignorance,
guiding our trembling steps toward clarity and peace.

Wisdom teaches us that no one is complete,
each of us is both a teacher and a student
in the boundless classroom of being.
Through dialogue—through this exchange of light and thought—
new windows of knowledge and kindness are opened,
and thirsty spirits are gently refreshed.

How beautiful it is to know that on this radiant path,
hearts and minds, like two distant rivers, merge together—
and every spark that shines in our hearts
becomes a torch lighting life’s road ever more brightly,
so that even in the world’s darkness, a shadow of hope might appear.

Wisdom goes beyond understanding and learning;
it is a still and glowing state of the heart,
a voiceless song, a melody winding through the core of being.
Listen to the music of existence:
in that sacred silence, you will hear the voice of wisdom,
calling you toward peace, serenity, and self-knowing.

And thus is wisdom in our way:

Wisdom
is a timeless light in awakened hearts,
a lamp shining upon the path of life,
a sacred and sturdy bridge
between hearts and thoughts—
lifting us from the dark of ignorance to the clarity of awareness,
teaching us that love and knowledge
are the two wings of the soul
flying toward the Infinite.
