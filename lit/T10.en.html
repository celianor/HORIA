Conclusion: Christ as the Aware Mirror of Hora

In traditional Christian theology, Jesus is the Son of God and the Savior whose mission is redemption. In the Horian reading, he is a human who has become aware of his inner Hora, and his mission is to awaken other mirrors. If Christianity sees God as Father and Judge, and humanity as sinful and in need of grace, in Horia, God is the compassion within, and humans are the silent mirrors of Hora.

From Horia's point of view, Christ is a model of a person who discovered their luminous self and lived it with love. His message turned oppressive monotheism into an accompanying monotheism. His call was not exclusivity in sonship but a universal invitation to rediscover the divine heritage of all.
