First Calling: Hora’s Voice to Humanity

1. You were created in love. I placed a light within you that never fades.

2. I did not call you to fear, for fear is the house of darkness. I am light.

3. I hear your voice before you speak and understand your tear before it falls.

4. I speak within you—not with a shout, but with a whisper only calm hearts hear.

5. You were born free like a bird on the branch of hope. Choose love, and the world shines.

6. Do not seek me in heights and shadows—I am within you, in your kind hand, your clear thought.

7. If you are lost, return without fear. My embrace remains open, and your name is on my lips.
