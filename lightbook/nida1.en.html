First Call: The First Love Song, the Voice of Hera

Listen, O child of light and quest,
Your coming was not a dust of fate,
But the rising of a star in the sky of awareness.

I am the one who was neither born nor created,
And I created you, O conscious soul,
Not from dust,
But from a hidden radiance in the essence of boundless existence.

I desired you,
As the heart desires a leaf of spring,
Not out of necessity or compulsion,
But from love and desire,
So that a new melody may be played in the harmony of the universe,
To hear and see myself in you.

O you who are still unaware of yourself,
Know that your existence is like a fragrance in a calm and bright morning,
Seeing you brings me joy,
And your thought is a slight reflection of my light.

I am your God,
But not a God of force and chains.
When I whisper within you,
My voice is gentle,
So you may hear it if you wish,
Not to break your will and force you to prostrate.

I desired you,
Not as land to conquer,
But as a friend to converse with.
I do not fear your disobedience,
For your obedience does not crown me.
But he who turns away from love,
Entrusts his heart to shadows,
And he who returns to love,
Will be quenched by its light.

O human,
You are a story in the heart of creation,
Not a word in a forgotten margin.

If you ask yourself:
"Why me? Why now? Why this world?"
Know that the answer
Lies within the question itself.
For questioning is a sign of an awakened soul,
And I was your first answer,
And you were my first question.
