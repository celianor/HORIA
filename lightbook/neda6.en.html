The Sixth Call: Love, Wisdom, and the Connection of Hearts

O you, in whom the breeze of my love blows,

Know that the first sign of Me in your heart is kindness.

Kindness is not a weak compassion,

But the highest form of wisdom.

Kindness is not a rootless gentleness,

But the knowledge of the heart,

Like a tree nourished at the spring of understanding existence.

O child of light,

Every time you offer a smile to a human being,

You gladden My heart.

And every time you soothe a soul,

You release the world from an invisible turmoil,

Yet the source of many sorrows.

Do not say: "What can I do?"

For a drop of love recreates the ocean.

And every hand extended with love

Is a gate opened toward the portals of light.

I made you to be like a candle,

That burns to light others,

Not a dead torch, surrendered to darkness.

Kindness flows from within,

Not from command.

And one who orders love

Creates only an appearance.

But one who teaches love through action

Invites the world into warmth.

O you, seeker of completeness,

Know that no one becomes whole by remaining alone.

In the connection of hearts, the human soul grows,

And I, God, dwell among united hearts.

Wisdom is true when it is empathic.

And thought shines when it lights the collective.

So consult, converse, listen.

For I placed a light in every servantâ€™s words,

And the one who listens only to himself

Misses half of creation.

No people grow under a single voice,

And no nation shines by selfishness.

Collective wisdom is a rain from many springs,

Which, together, make the land fertile.

Helping your neighbor is a silent prayer.

It needs no ritual, no display,

But within it, the heart is cleansed,

And the soul turns to God,

Without even saying His name.

O you who have a hand to give,

Know that I gave you that hand to share,

And a heart to understand that the other also suffers,

Perhaps hidden behind a smile.

Every time you lighten the burden of a human being,

Truly, you ease your own burden too.

And every time you share your bread,

A hidden light appears,

Which, returning to you, will transform your life.

Love, even in the desert,

For every seed of love will one day bloom in an unseen land.

And if you feel tired of kindness,

Know that you are only far from love,

And you are not tired,

For love itself dissolves all fatigue.

O My beloved child,

If you want to come closer to Me,

Build a home in the heart of another.

For My dwelling is in hearts,

Not on domes or minarets.

And whoever lights the heart of another,

Also lights My heart.
