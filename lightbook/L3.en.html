In praise of dialogue, collaboration, and the right to be heard

O human,
You are not alone on this winding path;
Destiny is a fabric woven by many hands.
You are not an isolated island,
Choosing your way of life in solitude,
But a link in the chain of existence;
You walk alongside others,
You see, listen, and build with them.

When hearts gather,
And tongues do not tremble in fear of silence,
And every thought is given space to blossom,
Wisdom will rise from the depths of all;
Not from one, but from a thousand searching souls.

I see opinion as a flame from the torch of liberty,
And deliberation as a lantern in the darkness of doubt.
And whoever rules alone,
Though crowned,
Is far from my path;
For to govern without the people's voice
Is not service, but domination;
Not leadership, but the erosion of souls.

O human,
Your opinion, if it rises from an awakened heart and enlightened mind,
Is a living essence,
By which tomorrow can be built,
Not by the sword, but by thought.
And if this jewel is taken from you,
It is as if they seek you silent,
Though your cry echoes in your throat.

Whoever disregards the people's voice,
Or steals it from them,
Is in conflict with me, with Horia;
Even if they speak my name,
Even if they sit upon a lofty throne.

Know that opinion is a shared dignity;
Dependent on neither age,
Nor lineage, nor gender, nor status.
Whether it comes from an untested youth,
Or a seasoned elder,
Whether it aligns with yours,
Or brings another view;
All count,
All are equal in worth.

And be aware:
In deliberation, seek only the truth,
Not shaky conveniences,
Nor false fears,
Nor the uproar of unthinking majorities.
Silence no idea out of fear of difference;
For salvation may lie in the lone voice,
And deliverance in a thought yet unborn.

O human,
If you want the light of your home to stay lit,
Let every glimmer shine,
Even the faintest.
And if you want your land to endure,
Honor the people‚Äôs opinion;
Not just when it serves you,
But especially when it does not.
