Book of Lights | Radiant Calls

The Third Call: Now You, Your Mirror, Song of Hura

O child of long times and shining paths,

Now you stand at the center,

Between what you were and what you will be.

You are neither the silent child nor the light’s creator,

But you carry a trace of both inside you.

This world where you walk and breathe,

Is not an exile nor a playground,

But a school for your soul,

And a mirror reflecting your being.

When I sent you to this place,

I put in your heart a light called “consciousness”,

It needs no book nor language,

It discerns the path of error,

And the friend from the foe.

This consciousness is like a stream,

If you do not block it with stones,

It stays clear.

And if you dig it,

You will see my reflection in it.

O you busy living,

Do not think life is a game without judge,

The wise sit inside you,

And their melody is your true voice.

Now you are in a time called life,

And life is another name for opportunity.

Opportunity to see, opportunity to know,

Opportunity to do good,

And opportunity to create your best self.

O beloved of potential,

I gave you this time,

Like a garden in four seasons:

In its spring desires bloom,

In its summer experiences ripen,

In its autumn judgments fall,

And in its winter, the silence of contemplation,

Preparing you for a new spring.

Do not waste your life,

Each moment is a precious jewel

That never returns once passed.

Have you not looked at the river?

If it does not flow, it does not stay clear.

Your life is the same,

In stagnation it becomes a puddle,

And in flowing it becomes a sea.

O the one who loves you,

And came from my love,

Do not say life is hard,

Adversity is the measure of your strength.

Like swords are polished in fire,

The heart is cleansed in pain.

Be good, not out of fear of punishment,

But out of longing for the light.

Be compassionate,

Compassion is a seed that grows in your field.

And when the long night comes,

Only your kindness will be your lamp.

O seeker of light,

Life is not to accumulate,

But to add existence.

Existence,

When it blooms from inside you.

I did not create you only to live,

If that were enough,

Your plant would suffice.

I gave you the soul,

To give meaning.

Every word that comes out of your mouth,

Builds a bridge or a wall.

Every step you take,

Is towards the light,

Or towards the shadow.

I take nothing from you,

Nor add anything to you,

Except what you do with your hands and your heart.

Life is a theatre for you;

I watch,

But the role is in your hands.

You are not just the actor,

But also its writer.

With every choice,

You write a new line in the book of creation.
