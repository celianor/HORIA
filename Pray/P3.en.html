Morning Prayer

O Hora, breath of the beginning, gentle wind of dawn,

You who whispered awakening in the morning light,

and hid kindness in the breeze —

O Hora, companion of the soul,

Now that the eye of my heart is open,

I call upon You,

to begin this new day

with a clear purpose and a peaceful heart.

I turn to You,

so that my steps may be filled with kindness,

and my words

become a soft melody in the heart of the world.

Shine upon me,

like sunlight on the leaf of a tree,

and nourish me,

like spring rain embracing a flower.

I entrust my heart to You,

so it may rest in truth,

and be drawn toward goodness.

O light of conscience, O spark of life,

do not let this day pass in forgetfulness,

nor let my moments fall into waste.

Purify my intentions,

adorn my lips with a smile,

and make my heart a home for kindness.

May I plant today

a seed that will blossom in tomorrow,

and spread a light that warms another’s soul.

May my gaze be radiant,

my words comforting,

my deeds full of compassion,

and my path bright.

Amen, O dawn of souls,

You whose remembrance at morning

is the first breath of life.
