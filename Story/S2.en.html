The Hidden Treasure

One day while playing, the children said, “Let’s hide a treasure!”
One said, “My necklace.”
Another said, “My little car.”
When it was Diane’s turn, she smiled and said:
“I’ve already hidden mine.”
Everyone asked, “What? Where?”
Diane said:
“A kind sentence. I told a friend: You have such a beautiful smile!
I hid it inside their heart.
So when they feel alone, they’ll find it.”

Some treasures can’t be seen,
but they’re never lost.
